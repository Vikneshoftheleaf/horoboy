How Your Daily Horoscope Based on Birth Chart Actually Works (The Secret Sauce!)

2. Progressions: Your Chart's Slow Burn of Evolution

While transits are the daily (or short-term) influences, **progressions** represent the slower, underlying currents of your psychological and spiritual development. They're like your **birth chart** symbolically growing up and maturing with you. These influences set broader themes over months or even years, filtering down to color your daily experiences. A **daily horoscope based on birth chart** will often consider these deeper currents to provide a more nuanced understanding of the energies at play.
